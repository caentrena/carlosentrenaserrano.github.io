I'm Carlos Entrena Serrano, a researcher interested in social media platforms and AI systems.

With a background in <i>Computer Science</i> and <i>Media and Communication Studies</i>, I explore how technology and society shape one another.

At the moment I am finishing my PhD at <a href="https://www.brussels-school.be/research/centre-digitalisation-democracy-and-innovation" target="_blank">Vrije Universiteit Brussel</a>. My research explores <i>how TikTok has reshaped what we understand as "social" media</i>. It moves away from the traditional idea of uploading our existing social networks online toward a model centered on <b>endless algorithmic recommendations from people we do not know much about.</b>
